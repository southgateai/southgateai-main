This file lists tenets.  Each has a short name used to refer to it in backlinks.

## Dualism

This is shorthand for there being more than just matter involved. ^dualism

## Quantum Wu

The minimal quantum wu to allow the dual ([[Tenets#^dualism]]) part to interact with the material world. ^quantum-wu

## No Many Worlds

Many world is rejected due to the vast proliferation of worlds and the difficulty in breaking symmetry (why am I this person and not another?). ^no-many-worlds
